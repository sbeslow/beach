Ecoli

E-coli is a bacteria found in the intestines of animals. The city monitors the amount of E-coli in the water as an indicator for the amount of fecal contamination. The US E.P.A recommends notifying the public when E. coli bacteria levels are above 235 CCE (Enterococci Calibrator Cell Equivalents per 100 milliliters of water) federal water quality Beach Action Value (BAV), which is 235. If it exceeds this level, there will be a Swim Advisory. When the level exceeds 1000 CE, the Park District issues a Swim Ban.
